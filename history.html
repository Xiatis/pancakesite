A Brief History

Ancient Times

Did you know that pancakes are one of the oldest foods in history? The Ancient Greeks and Romans ate pancakes sweetened with honey. They called them "Tiganitai".

Modern Day

Today, pancakes are famous all around the world. In America, they are thick and fluffy. In France, they are thin and called "Crêpes".

People usually eat them for breakfast with syrup, fruits, or chocolate.
